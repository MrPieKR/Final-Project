Find Your Next Adventure

Our goal is to make your camping experience much more enjoyable and simpler. We strive to provide a website that allows you to find the perfect camping location for your friends and family to enjoy. There are so many natural beauties in the state of Georgia and our goal is to help you decide where you want to stay. We provide information about the different campgrounds in Georgia while including that different activities and landmarks that surround the area.

CampGrounds

Forsyth KOA Journey

Forsyth KOA Journey is located at 414 Frontage Rd, Forsyth, GA 31029. It provides many forms of camping including rvs, tents, and log cabins. It also has many activities to do while camping including fishing, minigolf, and playgrounds. It also has a “Mining of treasure” where you can mine for different stones minerals and fossils.

Dames Ferry Park campground

Dames Ferry Park campground is located at 9546 GA-87, Juliette, GA 31046. It is a large campground of 3,600 acres that is located next to Lake Juliette, a lake formed by the Ocmulgee River. The activities provided here include fishing, boating, and aqua cycle rental. While not having many attractions it has many nearby attractions including other state parks and historical sites.

High Falls State Park River Campground

High Falls State Park River Campground is located at 76 High Falls Park Dr, Jackson, GA 30233. The park is located right next to the Towaliga River. The river provides many different activities to do here such as fishing, boating, hiking and so much more. While hiking you follow the beautiful river down the stream as you make your way down. It also provides beautiful land that is perfect for family picnics and parks nearby for children to play on.
